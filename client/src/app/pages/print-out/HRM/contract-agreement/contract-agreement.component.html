Part III
Duties and Obligation of the Employee
  1. The employee is obliged to devote all his/her time energy and ability to his/her work and undertake to discharge his duties and responsibilities conscientiously and diligently so that the objectives and aims of EMwA must be fully realized.
  2. When assigned as a lead trainer/ trainer, he/she should respect his/her team mates and participants/ trainees.
  3. The employee is obliged to be punctual, tolerance and create team spirit among his/her team mates and colleagues.
  4. The employee agrees and undertakes to abide by the current & new rules and regulations issued by the employer.
  5. The employee has the duty and obligation to keep well and utilize properly all property and equipment supplied to him/her by the employer.
  6. When he/she completes his/her duty or when he/she ceases to work, the employee has the obligations to return to the employer all property and equipment in the conditions in which he/initially received them from the employer.
  7. When he/she completes his/her duty or when he/she ceases to work, the employee has the obligations to return to the employer all property and equipment in the conditions in which he/initially received them from the employer.
  8. The employee shall refrain from all activities which may cause danger or harm to his/her colleagues, employer etc.
  9. The employee will be responsible to work full time on the regular working hours and days of EMwA. The normal working hours are: from 8:30a.m. to 12:30p.m in the morning and from 1:30-5:30 p.m. in the afternoon Monday through Thursday; and from 8:30 am-11:30 pm in the morning and from 1:30 pm - 5: 30 pm in the afternoon on Friday. However, the employee has a duty to work up to 48 hours in a week
  10. Whenever necessary, the employee may be instructed to work on weekends or holidays, outside normal duty hours and will be compensated in additional leave or additional pay depending on the situation and availability of funds in the project budget.
Consented by EMwA ED: __________________________________

 

Part IV
Obligations of Employer
  1. The employer has the obligation to provide office accommodation and office equipment necessary for the employee to perform his/her duties.
  2. The employer is obliged to pay the monthly salary on due date, and the employee will not be penalized for failure to do his/her work on grounds of the absence of the necessary equipment and accommodation which should have been made available to him/her on time.
  3. The employee is also entitled to have sick leave, annual leave and maternal leave in accordance with the Human Resources Manual of the employer.
  4. The employer will deduct pension
Part V
Termination of Contract
  1. This employment contract shall be terminated based up on the legal grounds stated on the association's HR manual and Ethiopian labor proclamation No 1156/2019 on this regard.
  2. This contract shall be terminated by either party (employer or employee) on the basis of the provisions of the Human Resources Manual of EMwA and the relevant laws.
Part VI
Governing laws and rules
  1. The job description, the Human Resource Manual and other rules/ policies of the employer are part and parcel of this employment contract.
